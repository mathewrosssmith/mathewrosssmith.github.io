 <summary><h3>My Power BI reports aren't showing</h3></summary>

 
 <p>If your dashboard tile looks like this...</p>

![PowerBISignInScreen](assets/img/PowerBISignInScreen.png)

<p>...but nothing happens when you click the "Sign-In" button, <span style="font-weight:bold">right-click</span> this button instead </p>
 
[![PowerBISignInButton](assets/img/PowerBISignInButton_100x45.png)](https://app.powerbi.com/home)
 
<p> and select "Open in new tab/window". (Alternatively, CTRL+click on windows or CMD+click on a Mac should accomplish the same thing.)
<br>
Once you are logged in on the new tab/window, the report should automatically sign-in and update here on your dashboard. To force this to occur immediately, simply refresh the tile by clicking the "..." in the top-right corner and selecting "Refresh" from the drop-down menu.
<br>
<span style="font-style: italic">Note: Once signed-in, you don't need to keep the other browser tab/window open in order to stay signed-in on the dashboard.</span></p>
