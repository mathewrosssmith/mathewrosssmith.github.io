## For the DM

### Resources

[Watabou's Procgen Arcana](https://watabou.github.io/): Useful for fleshing out details in the world specific to your run of the campaign. Alterion has hundreds of locations to explore. It won't all be detailed here.
Instead, you are encouraged to become familiar enough with the regions of the world, the lore, and the uniquenesses, and then introduce any number of characters, dungeons, townsfolk, natural disasters, or whatever
events and details make the exploration fun for your players. You may take inspiration from the magic items interactions rules in the spreadsheets below. Perhaps your adventurers investingate
a haunted house in southern Lulin, only to find some [pipes of haunting](https://www.dndbeyond.com/magic-items/4696-pipes-of-haunting) that kept playing themselves. Or maybe they find themselves in the midst
of a forest in Thendula when they discover that all the vegetation is awakened due to a long-lost [staff of the woodlands](https://www.dndbeyond.com/magic-items/4769-staff-of-the-woodlands). Or maybe your players
cause you to deviate from the prepared maps, such as by choosing to infiltrate the home of a government official to interrogate her about interference in the Games.

Alterion is designed to be a full, explorable world. The campaign is meant to maximize player agency and impact, without giving the DM hours of prepwork between sessions and without letting the world feel canned or generic.
Your players should have a hard time managing to interact with all of the cultures, the organizations, and the locations available for adventuring. The surges cause the entire mechanics of a region to feel like a totally
new world to explore with new challenges that the same old spells and tools won't always solve. Use these tools or don't, but you should feel at liberty to make Alterion whatever your players and you will enjoy.
Build a bastion and craft your own epic magic items. Have an entire sea-faring, pirate adventure. Rid a whole forest of monsters galore. Explore political intrigue and verbal warfare among the politicians. There should be a setting and a twist for every play style you are looking for, and every resource linked on this page is meant to make bringing that preferred style to life a reality.
