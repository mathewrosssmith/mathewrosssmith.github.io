# Catelog of DnD Resources Meant to be Made Available Free Online

## Alterion

Custom campaign world. Assets include maps (images and .map files), nation banners, articles, etc.

## Lightning Keep

Me and my brothers playing my first D&D campaign and anything we save from that, 
including AI generated images to animate our adventure, notes from the journeys, 
and links to spreadsheet tools we developed to help simplify dealing with an Artificer 
and custom crafting rules and tracking.
